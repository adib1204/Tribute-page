TUNKU ABDUL RAHMAN

Malaysia First Prime Minister

Quick Facts

  • Full name : Tunku Abdul Rahman Putra Al-Haj ibni Almarhum Sultan Abdul Hamid Halim Shah
  • Born : 8 February 1903 at Alor Setar Malaysia
  • Died : 6 December 1990 at Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia
  • Alma mater : St Catharine's College, Cambridge Inner Temple

Contributions

  • One of the prominent figures that fight for Malayan independence and gain it without any bloodshed
  • Became federation Malaya(later become Malaysia) Prime Minister from 1957 to 1970
  • Founder of Malaysia which include federation Malaya, Sabah, Sarawak and Singpore
  • Unified all races in Malaysia to live in harmony until now
  • Founder member of Organization of Islamic Conference
